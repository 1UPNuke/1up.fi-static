EISEI RULES:

Players take turn moving their own SATELLITES (pieces).

The goal of the game is to CAPTURE all the enemy satellites.

Each satellite can move one space either DIAGONALLY or HORIZONTALLY AND VERTICALLY based on their ORIENTATION.

MOVES:

RELOCATION: MOVE your satellite and ROTATE.

TAKEOVER: CAPTURE an enemy satellite by moving onto it.

RECOURSE: STAY in place and ROTATE.

SWAP: DIFFERENTLY ORIENTED satellites swap places WITHOUT ROTATING, satellites with SAME ORIENTATION swap places and ROTATE.
